See How Fasteink Works

1

Connect

Multiorders integrates with the most popular shopping carts, marketplaces and carriers - find yours and connect in a matter of minutes.

2

Import

After connecting your stores, import your products and orders and start managing them from a single dashboard.

3

Merge & Bundle

If you are selling across multiple sales channels, merge your stock and dashboard. Once an order is made on one sales channel, Multiorders will automatically deduct the stock from the others.

4

Watch orders gather

After connecting your sales channels, all your incoming orders will appear in a single place. Tag, filter, organize your orders, and check their status.

5

Automate your Workflow

Set up automation rules to automatically select carrier, service and packaging for the incoming orders, automatically tag and assign orders for your staff members.

6

Re-order your items

Get low stock notifications and automatically generate purchase orders to reorder items from your suppliers.

7

Email order documents to your customers

Generate pre-filled invoices, return forms and delivery notes and email them straight to your customers.

8

Print labels in seconds

Print labels individually or in batches up to 500 directly from Multiorders.
